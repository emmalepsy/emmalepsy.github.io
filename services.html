Common questions

How do your rates work?

I charge on a per project basis. That means you pay for the valuable marketing assets you get, not the hours that I spent creating them. My project rates range from $500 - $10,000. I require a 50% deposit when working with new clients.

Sometimes, an hourly rate makes sense for both of us. If we’re working together and the process is iterative (such as multiple edits and revisions for a website), I’ll charge an hourly rate. I also offer monthly retainers to clients who want marketing help on an hourly basis.

Do you have a waitlist?

Yes. There are lots of people that want the Emma factor. For this reason, I usually need three weeks of lead time when starting a new project. However, if you need me RIGHT NOW, I can make space for you (I charge a 20% rush fee to do so).

How do you deal with edits and revisions?

For each project, I offer one round of edits and revisions. That means that once you receive assets from me, you can go through and make suggestions. Once I receive your requests, I’ll get to work on making changes. This process simplifies things for both of us. You consolidate your revisions, and I get you the perfect piece as quickly as possible.

How do you manage large projects?

Large projects are fun. They require a lot of planning and communication to minimize stress, stay on task, and meet deadlines. When I work on a project with a large scope, I divide the work into three phases:

  1. Planning and research - You share what you need me to do. I read up on everything having to do with your business and your industry. I also interview you, your team, and your customers to get a sense of the subject matter. In the planning phase, I present exactly what the project will entail.

  2. Execution - I execute on what we’ve agreed upon. Execution includes content and copywriting, editing, and image sourcing.

  3. Revisions - I make revisions and changes based on what you want. From there, we’ll assess how the project when overall and double-check to make sure nothing is leftover.

This three phase process ensures that I deliver exactly what you want. We’ll be in touch throughout the process to make sure we’re on track.
